About Us

Greenfield is community-centered school committed to nurturing students to beome lifelog learners and responsible citizens. since its founding in 2010, our school has provided environment where academic excellence, creativity and character development go hand in hand

Our mission is to provide quality education that equips students with the skills and values needed to succeed in an ever-changing world. We focus on developing the whole child through academics, sports, arts, and community services
